# AI Chat Importer

**Back up, search, and archive your AI conversations — 100% locally, zero cloud upload.**

AI Chat Importer lets you import your ChatGPT, Claude, and DeepSeek conversation exports and search them privately in your browser. Your conversation data never leaves your device.

---

## What It Does

- **Import** your ChatGPT, Claude, and DeepSeek export files
- **Search** your full conversation history with full-text search
- **Browse** and filter by date, platform, and conversation
- **Archive** everything locally — no account, no upload, no cloud

---

## How It Works

1. Export your conversations from ChatGPT, Claude, or DeepSeek
2. Open [AI Chat Importer](https://ai-chat-importer.com) in your browser
3. Import your export file — processing happens entirely in your browser
4. Search and browse your full archive instantly

Your data is stored in your browser's local IndexedDB. Nothing is sent to any server.

## Privacy

AI Chat Importer is built on a local-first architecture:

- All import and search processing happens in your browser via JavaScript
- Conversation data is stored in IndexedDB on your device
- No conversation data is transmitted to any server
- No account required for the web app

Read more: [Privacy Architecture](https://ai-chat-importer.com/privacy-architecture)
